Vanessa-Mae Vanakorn Nicholson

Biography

Vanessa-Mae, born 27 October 1978, is a British violinist. She is known for her techno (and other) take on classical music.

She began her study of music very early. By the age of thirteen, she had recorded both Beethoven's and Tchaikovsky's violin concertos. Becoming the youngest soloist to do so, according to Guiness World Records

In, 1995, The Violin Player was released as her first pop-style album. By 2006, the Sunday Times Rich List 2006 ranked her as the wealthiest entertainer under 30 in the UK.

ClassicFM complied a list in 2017 of best selling classical albums over the last 25 years. Vanessa-Mae had three entries. The Classical Album 1 ranked 244, Storm reached 135, and her debut album, The Violin Player, was 76th. The site claims that her total sales make "her the biggest-selling solo violinist in the chart".
